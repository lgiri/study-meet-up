Study Meet Up

If you are here to form study groups, head over to the Schools/Colleges tab on the header and select the school/college you want to form a study group in. On that page, fill out the form and access the contact information of other students as well. If you would like to host an online study session, head over to the Postings tab and make an announcement with specific descriptions and links for your study session so that others can join. Happy studying!
